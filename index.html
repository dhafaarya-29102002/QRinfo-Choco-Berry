Choco Berry

Classic Roast Edition

Our Coffee

Experience the classic and pleasent taste of Dark Chocolate with Dried Red Berry tasted coffee.

Choco Berry is one of the product line in heritage coffee classic roast edition line up. It's offer a pleasent and classical sensation of coffee that Malang has to offer. With its berry like acidity and sweetness and dark chocolate like sweetness and pleasent bitter, Choco Berry could gives you a strong coffee sensation with some senses of fruity twist.

Coffee source

UB Forest Karangploso

Processing Method

Choco Berry is a mix variety Arjuna coffee (mostly komasti) which is processed using Immersed anaerobic natural process with addition of lactic fermentation process. This process is performed to ensure the anaerobic condition and increase the availibility of enzymatic reaction, enabling the green bean to develop various flavor precursor which is transformed into a dried berry, grape, dark chocolate like flavor compound during roasting process
